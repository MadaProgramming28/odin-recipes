Pasta Recipe

Description

This pasta recipe is simple, quick, and full of flavor, making it a perfect meal for lunch or dinner. It uses fresh ingredients and can be customized with your favorite vegetables or sauces.

Ingredients

  • 200g pasta (spaghetti, penne, or your choice)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cup spinach leaves
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Parmesan cheese for serving
  • Fresh basil (optional)

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large pan, heat olive oil over medium heat and add minced garlic.
  3. Cook the garlic for about 1 minute until fragrant.
  4. Add the cherry tomatoes and cook for 3-4 minutes until they start to soften.
  5. Add the spinach leaves and cook until wilted.
  6. Add the cooked pasta to the pan and mix well to combine with the vegetables.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Serve with freshly grated Parmesan cheese and fresh basil if desired.
