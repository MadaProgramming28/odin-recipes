Pizza Recipe

Description

This homemade pizza recipe will give you a crispy crust with delicious melted cheese and your favorite toppings. It's easy to make and perfect for dinner with friends or family.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 1 packet dry yeast
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Toppings of your choice (pepperoni, vegetables, etc.)

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix warm water, sugar, and yeast. Let it sit for 5 minutes.
  2. Add flour, salt, and olive oil. Mix and knead until you get a smooth dough.
  3. Cover and let it rise for 1 hour.
  4. Preheat your oven to 220°C (425°F).
  5. Roll out the dough on a floured surface.
  6. Place the dough on a baking sheet, add tomato sauce and spread evenly.
  7. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese and add your desired toppings.
  8. Bake for 15-20 minutes until the crust is golden and cheese melted.
  9. Remove from oven, let it cool slightly, then slice and enjoy your pizza!
