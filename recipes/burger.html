Burger Recipe

Description

This homemade burger recipe will give you a juicy, flavorful burger with melted cheese, fresh vegetables, and your favorite sauces. Perfect for a quick lunch or a weekend meal with friends.

Ingredients

  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 egg
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 burger buns
  • 4 slices of cheese
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Tomato slices
  • Pickles
  • Ketchup and mustard
  • Oil for cooking

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix ground beef, chopped onion, egg, salt, and pepper.
  2. Shape the mixture into 4 equal burger patties.
  3. Heat a pan with a little oil over medium-high heat.
  4. Cook the patties for about 4-5 minutes on each side, until browned and cooked through.
  5. In the last minute of cooking, place a slice of cheese on each patty to melt.
  6. Toast the burger buns lightly in the pan or in the oven.
  7. Assemble the burgers: place lettuce on the bottom bun, add the patty with cheese, tomato slices, pickles, and your favorite sauces.
  8. Cover with the top bun and serve warm.
